from

$$ \text{fib}(n) = \frac{\phi^n - \psi^n}{\sqrt{5}} $$

and

$$ \text{fib}(n) = \text{fib}(n-1) + \text{fib}(n-2) $$

follows

$$ \frac{\phi^n - \psi^n}{\sqrt{5}} = \frac{\phi^{n-1} - \psi^{n-1}}{\sqrt{5}} + \frac{\phi^{n-2} - \psi^{n-2}}{\sqrt{5}} $$

$ \times \sqrt{5} $

$$ \phi^n - \psi^n = \phi^{n-1} - \psi^{n-1} + \phi^{n-2} - \psi^{n-2} $$

$$ \phi^n - \psi^n = \frac{\phi^n}{\phi} - \frac{\psi^n}{\psi} + \frac{\phi^n}{\phi^2} - \frac{\psi^n}{\psi^2} $$

$$ \phi^n - \psi^n = \phi^n \Big( \frac{1}{\phi} + \frac{1}{\phi^2} \Big) - \psi^n \Big( \frac{1}{\psi} + \frac{1}{\psi^2} \Big) $$

with $ \phi = \frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2} $ and $ \psi = \frac{1-\sqrt{5}}{2} $

$$ \phi^n - \psi^n = \phi^n \Bigg( \frac{1}{\frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2}} + \frac{1}{{\Big(\frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2}\Big)}^2} \Bigg) - \psi^n \Bigg( \frac{1}{\frac{1-\sqrt{5}}{2}} + \frac{1}{{{\Big(\frac{1-\sqrt{5}}{2}}\Big)}^2} \Bigg) $$

$$ \phi^n - \psi^n = \phi^n \Bigg( \frac{2}{1+\sqrt{5}} + \frac{4}{(1+\sqrt{5})^2} \Bigg) - \psi^n \Bigg( \frac{2}{1-\sqrt{5}} + \frac{4}{(1-\sqrt{5})^2} \Bigg) $$

$$ \phi^n - \psi^n = \phi^n \Bigg( \frac{2(1+\sqrt{5})+4}{(1+\sqrt{5})^2} \Bigg) - \psi^n \Bigg( \frac{2(1-\sqrt{5})+4}{(1-\sqrt{5})^2} \Bigg) $$

$$ \phi^n - \psi^n = \phi^n \Bigg( \frac{2+2\sqrt{5}+4}{1+2\sqrt{5}+5} \Bigg) - \psi^n \Bigg( \frac{2-2\sqrt{5}+4}{1-2\sqrt{5}+5} \Bigg) $$

$$ \phi^n - \psi^n = \phi^n \Bigg( \frac{6+2\sqrt{5}}{6+2\sqrt{5}} \Bigg) - \psi^n \Bigg( \frac{6-2\sqrt{5}}{6-2\sqrt{5}} \Bigg) $$

$$ \phi^n - \psi^n = \phi^n - \psi^n $$

q.e.d.
